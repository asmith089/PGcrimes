User Manual

PG Crime is a web-application with a search, filter, data analysis, and data visualization functionality.


  • Home: On the Home page, users can search through Prince George's county crime. Type in a street name and click Go to see results. Click on the filter beside the search bar to see a drop down list of crime types. Click on the crime type you wish to only see and click Go to see results.

  • About: On the About page, users can learn about what PG Crime is and who is the team behind it.

  • Data: On the Data page, users can explore the data behind different crime types within Prince George's county. The visual bar graph identifies each crime type and users can hover over the bar to show the exact amount of crime entries there are for that specific crime type. In addition the data visual, users can interact with the data by adding to it. Based on their personal experience, users can enter the type of crime that they are concerned about by clicking on the entry bar and typing what they wish to add to the graph. Once the Add button is clicked, refresh the page to see the updated data visual.
